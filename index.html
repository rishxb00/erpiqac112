What is ERP & IQAC?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) integrates institutional processes such as academics, finance, and HR. IQAC (Internal Quality Assurance Cell) ensures continuous improvement in education quality.

By integrating ERP with IQAC, institutions can automate quality data collection, ensure compliance with standards, and drive better decision-making.

Key ERP Modules for IQAC

Faculty Tracking

Monitor teaching performance, achievements, and contributions.

Student Feedback

Collect, analyze, and report feedback for quality improvement.

Audit Reports

Generate audit-ready documentation and NAAC reports instantly.
