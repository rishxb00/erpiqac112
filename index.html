What is ERP & IQAC?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) integrates institutional processes such as academics, finance, and HR. IQAC (Internal Quality Assurance Cell) ensures continuous improvement in education quality.

By integrating ERP with IQAC, institutions can automate quality data collection, ensure compliance with standards, and drive informed decision-making.

Key ERP Modules for IQAC

Faculty Tracking

Monitor teaching performance, academic achievements, and faculty contributions.

Student Feedback

Collect, analyze, and act upon student feedback to improve quality and engagement.

Audit Reports

Generate audit-ready documentation and automate NAAC report creation.
